# “Explain Like I’m New to This Literature” Prompt

Use this when the article feels difficult or full of unfamiliar concepts.

```text
Explain this article as if I am new to this literature.

Please:

1. Define the key concepts in simple language.
2. Explain the research question without jargon.
3. Explain why scholars care about this question.
4. Summarize the main argument step by step.
5. Explain the method in simple terms.
6. Explain the findings clearly.
7. Identify which parts are most important to understand.
8. Tell me what background knowledge would help me understand this article better.
```
