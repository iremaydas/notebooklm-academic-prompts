# Contribution Prompt

Use this when the article feels technically clear, but you are unsure why it matters.

```text
Help me understand the contribution of this article.

Please answer:

1. What gap in the literature does the article identify?
2. What does the article claim previous studies missed or misunderstood?
3. What is the article’s main contribution?
4. Is the contribution theoretical, empirical, methodological, conceptual, or policy-related?
5. What changes after we read this article?
6. Who should care about this article and why?
7. What is the “so what?” in plain language?
8. What would be lost if this article did not exist?
9. Give me a 2–3 sentence version of the contribution.
```
