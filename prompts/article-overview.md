# Article Overview Prompt

Use this when you are reading an article for the first time.

```text
I uploaded an academic article. Please summarize it in a structured way.

Answer the following:

1. What is the main research question?
2. What is the main argument?
3. What is the dependent variable?
4. What are the main independent variables, explanations, or mechanisms?
5. What theory or literature does the article engage with?
6. What data, cases, or evidence does the author use?
7. What is the method or research design?
8. What are the main findings?
9. What is the article’s main contribution?
10. Why does this article matter? 

Please write clearly and avoid overly generic summaries.
```
