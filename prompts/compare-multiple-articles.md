# Compare Multiple Articles Prompt

Use this when NotebookLM has multiple uploaded articles.

```text
Compare the uploaded articles.

Answer the following:

1. What research question does each article ask?
2. What is the main argument of each article?
3. How are the articles’ arguments similar?
4. How are their arguments different?
5. Do they use similar or different methods?
6. Do they rely on similar or different theoretical frameworks?
7. Do they reach compatible, conflicting, or complementary conclusions?
8. Which articles are most convincing, and why?
9. Are there any articles that are weaker, less clear, or less persuasive? Why?
10. How could these articles be grouped together in a literature review?
11. What broader debate, literature, or research agenda connects them?
12. What are the main points of agreement and disagreement across the articles?
13. What gaps remain after reading these articles together?
14. Write a synthesis paragraph that connects all of the articles.
```
