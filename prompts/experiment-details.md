# Experiment Details Prompt

Use this for experiments.

```text
This article includes an experiment. Please explain the experiment in detail.

Answer the following:

1. What type of experiment is it?
2. Who are the participants or subjects?
3. How were participants assigned to conditions?
4. What are the treatment and control conditions?
5. What exactly is manipulated?
6. What is the outcome variable?
7. How is the outcome measured?
8. What hypotheses are tested?
9. What are the main experimental results?
10. Are there heterogeneous treatment effects? If yes, by what variables?
11. What validity concerns should I consider?
   - internal validity
   - external validity
   - construct validity
   - ecological validity
12. Explain the experiment as if I needed to describe it in a seminar.
```
