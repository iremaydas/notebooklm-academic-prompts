# “What Should I Remember?” Prompt

Use this after reading when you want a memory-friendly summary.

```text
What should I remember from this article six months from now?

Please give me:

1. One-sentence summary
2. Main research question
3. Main argument
4. Key finding
5. Main contribution
6. Best quote or idea to remember, paraphrased unless directly necessary
7. Why this article matters
8. How I might use it in my own research
```
