# Best All-Purpose Prompt

Use this when you do not know exactly what you need yet.

```text
I uploaded an academic article. Help me understand it deeply.

Please provide:

1. A concise summary
2. The main research question
3. The main argument
4. The theory and causal mechanism
5. The data and methods
6. The main findings
7. The contribution to the literature
8. The “so what?” of the article
9. Strengths and weaknesses
10. Critical questions I could ask
11. How this article connects to broader debates
12. How could I use this article in my own research notes

Please be specific and avoid generic descriptions.
```
