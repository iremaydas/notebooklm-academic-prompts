# Methods Translation Prompt

Use this when the method is difficult, and you want it translated into simple language.

```text
Translate the methods section into plain language.

Please explain:

1. What the authors are trying to estimate, measure, or test
2. What data they use
3. What each main variable means
4. Why they chose this method
5. What the model or design does
6. What assumptions are necessary
7. How to interpret the main results
8. What the tables or figures show
9. What I should be careful about when interpreting the results

Avoid unnecessary technical jargon, but do not oversimplify.
```
