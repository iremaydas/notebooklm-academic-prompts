# Critical Evaluation Prompt

Use this after you understand the article and want to think more critically.

```text
Critically evaluate this article.

Please discuss:

1. What is most convincing about the article?
2. What is least convincing?
3. Are the research question and argument clearly connected?
4. Are the concepts well defined?
5. Are the measures valid?
6. Is the method appropriate for the question?
7. Are there alternative explanations?
8. Are the findings overstated?
9. What are the limitations of the article?
10. What would improve the article?
11. What future research could build on this article?

Please be specific and avoid generic criticism.
```
