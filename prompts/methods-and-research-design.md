# Methods and Research Design Prompt

Use this when you mainly care about how the study is conducted.

```text
Analyze the methods and research design of this article.

Please answer:

1. What type of design is used? 

2. What is the unit of analysis?

3. What is the sample or case selection strategy?

4. What are the main variables, concepts, or treatments?

5. How are the key concepts measured?

6. What is the identification strategy?

7. What assumptions does the method rely on?

8. What are the strengths of this design?

9. What are the weaknesses or possible threats to validity?

10. If I wanted to replicate or extend this study, what information would I need?
```
