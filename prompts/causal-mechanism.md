# Causal Mechanism Prompt

Use this when the article makes a causal argument, and you want to understand how X leads to Y.

```text
Identify and explain the causal mechanism in this article.

Answer the following:

1. What is the main cause?
2. What is the main outcome?
3. What mechanism connects the cause to the outcome?
4. What are the intermediate steps in the argument?
5. What support does the author provide for the mechanism?
6. Is the mechanism directly tested or mostly assumed?
7. Are there alternative mechanisms?
8. What would we expect to observe if the mechanism is correct?
9. What would weaken the mechanism?
10. Explain the causal relationship using this format:

X → mechanism step 1 → mechanism step 2 → Y
```
