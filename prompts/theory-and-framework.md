# Theory and Framework Prompt

Use this when you want to understand the theoretical structure of the article.

```text
Explain the theoretical framework of this article.

Please identify:

1. The main theoretical puzzle
2. The core concepts
3. The causal mechanism
4. The assumptions behind the argument
5. The scope conditions
6. The hypotheses or expectations
7. The literature the article responds to
8. Whether the theory challenges, extends, or is in line with existing work
9. Any diagrams, typologies, or conceptual distinctions used in the article
10. The simplest version of the theory in 3–5 sentences

Then explain what is theoretically new or important about the article.
```
